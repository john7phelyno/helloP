Privacy Policy for Hello Pinyin

Hello Pinyin is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our Google Play app. By using our app, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal data when you use our app. We only use the following permissions:

Audio: We use your device's microphone to record your pronunciation of Chinese words, so that we can provide you with feedback on your pronunciation.

Camera: We use your device's camera to scan QR codes, which you can use to access additional content in our app.

Internet: We use the internet to download content and updates for our app, as well as to play audio files.

How We Use Your Information
We do not collect or use any personal information about you when you use our app. We only use the information collected through your device's microphone, camera, and internet connection to provide you with the services and features of our app.

Information Sharing
We do not share any information about you with third parties. We do not collect or use any personal information about you, and we do not share any data with any third-party services or advertisers.

Security
We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect through your device's microphone, camera, and internet connection. We have implemented reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
